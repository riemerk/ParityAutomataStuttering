We try to use a consistent notation during this thesis. We give the following list of symbols and abbreviations frequently used in this thesis\\

% \[
%  \begin{array}{ll}
%    \phi, \psi,\chi & \mutl \text{ formula}\\
%  \end{array}
% \]
% ]
\begin{tabular}{ll}
 $\phi, \psi,\chi$ & \hyperref[def:mutempform]{$\mutl$-formula}\\
 $p,q,r,\dots$ & Free variables\\
 $x,y,z$ & Bound variables\\
 $Sf(\phi)$ & \hyperref[def:subformulas]{Subformulas of $\phi$}\\
 $FV(\phi)$ and $BV(\phi)$ & \hyperref[def:boundandfree]{Free and bound variables of $\phi$}\\
 $\mathcal{B}, \mathcal{E}$ & \hyperref[def:pargame]{Parity Game}\\
 $\mathcal{B}@a$ & Initialized parity game\\
 $\mathbb{B}$ & Board, or game graph of a parity Game\\
 $a,b,c$ & states in an automata, nodes in a parity formula or positions in a parity game\\
 $f$ & \hyperref[def:winning]{Strategy}\\
 $\mathbb{T}_a^f$ and $\mathbb{B}_a^f$ & \hyperref[def:strattree]{Strategy tree} and \hyperref[def:stratgraph]{Strategy graph}\\
 $\A,\A^n, \A^a$ & General, respectively \hyperref[def:NPA]{nondeterministic} and alternating $\omega$-automata.\\
 $\Omega$ & Parity map.\\
 $\Sigma$ & Alphabet\\
 $v$ & Letter in Alphabet, mostly.\\
 $A$ & Set of states\\
 $\rho$, $\rightarrow$, $\twoheadrightarrow$ & \hyperref[def:NPArun]{Runs of automata}\\
 $\delta$ & Transition function\\
 $\G$ & \hyperref[def:linparform] (Linear) parity formula
\end{tabular}
